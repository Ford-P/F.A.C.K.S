Symptoms

The most common symptoms of the coronavirus are:

  • Fever
  • Dry cough
  • Fatigue

Other less common symptoms of the coronavirus include:

  • Loss of taste or smell
  • Nasal congestion
  • Conjunctivitis
  • Sore throat
  • Headache
  • Muscle or joint pain
  • Different types of skin rash
  • Nausea or vomiting
  • Diarrhea
  • Chills or dizziness

Symptoms of severe COVID-19 include:

  • Shortness of breath
  • Loss of appetite
  • Confusion
  • Persistent pain or pressure in the chest
  • High temperature (above 38 °C)

If you or someone close to you have any of the main symptoms, get a test as soon as possible. Stay self-isolated until you get a result.
