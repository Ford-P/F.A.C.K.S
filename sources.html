How and why to prevent over buying unnecessary groceries and supplies

It's as simple as this; you are not the only person on the planet. When the world found out about the pandemic, everyone went into a sudden frenzy thinking the world is going to end and the last bit of toilet paper you were going to ever get is what was currently in stock at ur local store. By doing this, people just made other peoples lives more difficult by hoarding and buying more than they need. Learning to be more considerate when doing your shopping makes a massive difference, especially for those less able to go about buying things in bulk. You would think that by buying things in bulk it would be easier to stay in quarantine but what about those who didn't get a chance to get a pack of toilet roll that day? Heres an idea: do the shopping like its any other day! This way, everyone has a more equal chance of having what they need during the pandemic.

What do I actually need during this pandemic?

Other than a basic grasp of knowing what 2 metres apart means when social distancing; what you need is:

⁍ A mask
- it can be a medical/surgical mask or a cloth mask. As long as it covers your mouth and nose appropriately at all times.

⁍ Anti-bacterial gel
- use whenever you get in contact with anything that someone else uses other than yourself

⁍ The NHS Covid 19 App
- This app helps you to identify whether you or someone else you have came in contact with has Covid.

For more information and help downloading the app, click here

Typically thats all you need when needing to leave the house for essential errands as the majority of the time you should be at home.

Shops and Supermarkets

You can get your supplies at any of your nearby newsagents or supermarkets which could vary from:

⁍ ASDA
⁍ Tesco
⁍ Sainsbury
⁍ Morrisons

In this climate, 99.9% of the shops that you will encounter will probably be selling these.
